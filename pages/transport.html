Transport Guidelines

Important information for a smooth commuting experience

For Parents

  • Punctuality

    Students must report at the bus stop 5 minutes before scheduled arrival.

  • Safety Confirmation

    Ensure your ward has their ID card and bus pass at all times.

  • Change Requests

    Route or stop changes require a formal request 1 month in advance.

  • Official Communication

    All transport-related queries should be sent via the official college portal/email only.

For Students

  • Dress Code

    Proper nursing uniform and polished shoes are mandatory for bus travel.

  • Identification

    College ID card with the designated bus number must be clearly visible.

  • Bus Conduct

    Eating, drinking, or loud music is strictly prohibited to maintain decorum.

  • Discipline

    Maintain strict discipline; any misbehavior may lead to immediate cancellation of service.

College Timings

Buses are meticulously scheduled to ensure all students arrive at the campus by 8:30 AM daily for their clinical and academic sessions.

Routes are strategically designed to minimize transit time and provide a comfortable, stress-free journey before the day starts.
