About SNS iHub for Nursing

SNS iHub is an initiative by SNS Institutions to foster healthcare startups by providing a prototyping space immersed in an ambience of creativity and our patented Design Thinking framework specifically for the nursing field.

The center helps nursing students identify patient care needs and solve them using our AI-enhanced design thinking approach, mentored by experts from both medical and technology industries.
