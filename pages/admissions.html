Admission Process

1

Enquiry & Registration

Submit your enquiry through our website or visit the campus to register for the B.Sc. Nursing program.

2

Eligibility Verification

Our admissions team will verify your 10+2 marks (PCB aggregate) based on the Tamil Nadu Dr. M.G.R. Medical University norms.

3

Counseling & Interview

Shortlisted candidates will be called for a personal interview and counseling session to discuss career goals.

4

Final Admission

Upon successful completion of the interview and verification, final seats are allocated based on merit and university regulations.
